Our Programs

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art that focuses on ground fighting and submission techniques. Originating from judo and developed in Brazil, BJJ emphasizes leverage, grip, and position to control opponents, making it an effective self-defense method regardless of size or strength.

At Be Fit Gym, our BJJ classes provide a safe and supportive environment where practitioners can learn, practice, and hone their skills. Our experienced instructors guide you through techniques, drills, and sparring sessions to build both your physical abilities and strategic thinking.

Muay Thai
Muay Thai, also known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," is a striking martial art from Thailand that utilizes punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Known for its rigorous and effective techniques, Muay Thai is excellent for building cardiovascular fitness, strength, and agility.

At Be Fit Gym, our Muay Thai classes cater to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors are dedicated to teaching the fundamentals and advanced techniques of Muay Thai in a structured and engaging environment, ensuring that you develop both your physical and mental skills.

We offer a diverse range of classes to meet the needs of all our members. Our programs are designed to cater to different skill levels and interests, ensuring a fulfilling experience for everyone.
